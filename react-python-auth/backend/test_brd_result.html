Validations & Acceptance Criteria

  1. Enforce Validations.
  2. Enforce Identity & KYC: mandatory demographics, document number formats, OCR confidence thresholds, liveness/selfie match, sanctions/PEP clearing before activation.
  3. Enforce Payments: beneficiary validation, risk/velocity checks, 2FA/OTP or 3DS where applicable, amount within customer/product limits, idempotency on submission, success callback before posting.
  4. Enforce Accounts & Cards: unique customer/account identifiers, address and PIN policies, card controls consistent with scheme/region.
  5. Enforce blocked statuses prevent transactions.
  6. Enforce Deposits/Loans: eligibility and limit checks, tenor/amount ranges, premature closure penalties rules, EMI and interest calculations verified.
  7. Enforce Security & Compliance: RBAC enforced for every sensitive action, audit trail for create/read/update/delete, encryption in transit/at rest, session timeout and device binding, WCAG 2.1 AA for customer screens.
  8. Enforce Data Integrity: totals and balances reconcile after each posting.
  9. Enforce failure paths roll back cleanly with clear user messaging.
